Tax-Deductable Girls

The Wash U sailing team is partially funded by the sports club board which allows for traveling to regattas and putting on other functions such as practices. Remaining costs are covered by member dues. If you would like to support the team financially and help lower these dues, please contact us at wu.sailing@wustl.edu or follow this link to donate through the leading together campaign. Please enter "Sports Club Board - Sailing" into the field "My Designation."

Equipment Donation

Our team practices with 2 420-class sailboats. We are looking to expand our capacity by purchasing additional equipment. If you have any new or used sailing equipment to donate, please email us at wu.sailing@wustl.edu
