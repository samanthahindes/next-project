EVENTS we attend

Types of Events

  • Regular Meetings: learn general information about the club
  • Dry Land Meetings: learn about racing fundamentals
  • Competition: travel throughout the midwest to compete
  • Social: get to know other team members

The executive board attempts to organize a diverse set of opportunities for WashU sailors of all skill levels. We make it a priority to lower barriers to sailing. Because of this, we sailing team is a low-commitment membership. No event is required to our members. Throughout the year, we also have many social events.
